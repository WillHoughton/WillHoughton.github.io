What is emotional intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is the ability for a person to understand both their own emotions and others, using their understanding to guide their thinking and behaviour around others.

How is it different to IQ?

Emotional Quotient is significantly different from Intelligence Quotient, while EQ is how a person understands his or her own emotions and others around them to inluence their own decisions and behaviour IQ indicates a persons intelligence level.

Why is it important?

Emotional Intelligence is important as it enables effective team work and collaborative learning, if co-workers have a high EQ they can work together in harmony with little conflict increasesing productivity.
